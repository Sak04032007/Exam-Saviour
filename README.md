#  Celeritas
### *Agentic RAG Exam Assistant for CSE Students*

**Celeritas** is a high-performance study tool built with **Llama 3.3-70B** and **LangChain**. Unlike standard AI chats, it uses an agentic workflow to ensure your study sessions are fast, accurate, and strictly grounded in your university notes.

---

##  Core Features
*   **Agentic Routing:** Automatically switches between your **PDF Notes**, **Web Search**, and **LLM Knowledge**.
*   **Zero-Hallucination Guard:** A secondary grading agent verifies if answers are truly in your notes.
*   **Professor Personas:** Specialized modes for **Data Science, AI/ML, Cyber Security,** and **Data Structures**.
*   **Viva Voice Mode:** Simulates a strict external examiner to prep you for oral exams.

---

##  Interactive Tools
1. **Dynamic Exam Countdown:** Set your date and track the remaining time.
2. **Custom Syllabus Tracker:** Input your specific university topics to stay organized.
3. **Instant Flashcard Generator:** Create quick-study cards from your uploaded PDF.

---

##  Usage
1. **Upload** your course PDF in the sidebar.
2. **Input** your specific syllabus topics in the tracker.
3. **Set** your exam date for the live countdown.
4. **Chat** with the Professor to master your subjects.
